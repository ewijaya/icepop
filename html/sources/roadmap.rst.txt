Development roadmap
-------------------

This document outline's ICEPOP intended development path.

.. WARNING::
   This information is subject to change, and should not 
   be used as life- or career-altering decisions!

ICEPOP 1.x
==========

In this version we will emphasize the completion of DEGs cell population
analysis at its entirety. It also includes non-DEGs (raw expression)
deconvolution method.

ICEPOP 2.x
==========

Implement gene network analysis for cell population.
